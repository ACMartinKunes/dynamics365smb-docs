## Service updates

[!INCLUDE [prod_short](includes/prod_short.md)] online is a service that consists of a Microsoft-maintained platform and business functionality. Both business functionality and service components are monitored continuously and updated as appropriate. All updates that Microsoft applies to [!INCLUDE [prod_short](includes/prod_short.md)] online are also shipped with the subsequent cumulative update for [!INCLUDE [prod_short](includes/prod_short.md)] on-premises. For an example, see [Update 17.3 for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central online 2020 release wave 2](whatsnew/whatsnew-update-17-3.md). For more information, see [Service Overview for Business Central Online](/dynamics365/business-central/dev-itpro/service-overview).
